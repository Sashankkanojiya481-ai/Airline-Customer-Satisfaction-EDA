# ✈️ Airline Customer Satisfaction Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes airline passenger satisfaction using a real-world dataset containing over **129,000 passenger records**. The objective of this analysis is to identify the key factors that influence customer satisfaction and provide business recommendations for improving airline services.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The main goals of this project are:

* To analyze customer satisfaction levels.
* To identify factors affecting passenger experience.
* To understand the impact of delays, seat comfort, and onboard services.
* To provide business recommendations based on data-driven insights.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

* Dataset: Airline Passenger Satisfaction Dataset
* Total Records: **129,880**
* Total Features: **24**

The dataset contains information related to:

* Customer satisfaction
* Flight delays
* Travel class
* Seat comfort
* In-flight entertainment
* Online services
* Customer demographics

---

## 🔍 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The following analyses were performed:

* Data cleaning and preprocessing
* Missing value analysis
* Customer satisfaction distribution
* Class-wise satisfaction analysis
* Delay impact analysis
* Age distribution analysis
* Service quality analysis
* Correlation and business insights

---

## 📈 Key Findings

* Business class passengers have the highest satisfaction rate.
* Economy class passengers show a higher dissatisfaction rate.
* Flight delays significantly affect customer satisfaction.
* Seat comfort and in-flight entertainment strongly influence passenger experience.
* Customers aged between 25–45 years show higher dissatisfaction levels.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

* Airlines should reduce arrival and departure delays.
* Economy class services should be improved.
* Better seat comfort and in-flight entertainment should be provided.
* Customer service and online support systems should be optimized.

## 🚀 Conclusion
## Final Business Conclusion

After analyzing 129,880 airline passenger records, we found that customer satisfaction is mainly influenced by flight delays, seat comfort, in-flight entertainment, and travel class. Business class passengers show significantly higher satisfaction levels, while economy passengers experience higher dissatisfaction rates. Reducing delays and improving onboard services can substantially increase overall customer satisfaction.
